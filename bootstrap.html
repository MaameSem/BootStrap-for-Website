This is my music website.

On this website, I share with you my musical journey so far and recommend great resources that have helped me along the way.

How it started for me.

I loved to sing when I was very much young, say from age 3. However, by the age of 8, I started leaning more towards musical instruments. I believe the toy musical instruments my Dad bought for my brother and I triggered it. It began with a passion for the keyboard. I just loved the arrangement of the keys and loved watching live bands play.

  • Prince
  • Ephraim
  • Osei
